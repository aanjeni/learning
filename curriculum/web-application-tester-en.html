Learning objective:
This course outlines federal standards and guidelines on web accessibility and emphasizes the requirements, roles and responsibilities involved in compliance with this standard. Participants will learn how to assess and apply Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0 consistently across Canada.ca web pages.

How to audit: accessible QA for sites, apps, and documents

Learning objective:

Instructional videos on the European standard for accessibility requirements in public procurement of ICT (Information Communication Technology) products and services. These videos are sponsored by Microsoft and produced by Funka

  • Accessibility quality assurance
  • Accessibility supported technologies
  • Standard controls vs. custom controls
  • Strategies of persons with disabilities in using web solutions
  • Interoperability and compatibility issues
  • Testing with assistive technologies
